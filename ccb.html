California Cryobank

Scalable e-commerce solutions for biotech and reproductive services.

role/

Lead UX & Visual Design

agency/

Basis

industry/

Life Sciences & Healthcare

years/

2015–2019

overview/

California Cryobank (CCB) is a worldwide leader in biotech and reproductive services with 7 key businesses under it’s umbrella. As lead UX designer at Basis, I worked directly with its marketing department and product owners for 4 years to rebuild its properties and create a unified persona across all businesses, allowing the company to appeal to more clients, optimize e-commerce conversions, and easily scale through acquisitions.

Optimizations & Efficiencies Bring Results

The redesign and optimization began with the Sperm Client site — California Cryobank’s foundation and core business. In 2014, the site was not mobile-responsive, came with outdated gray gradients, and had flat imagery that we found did not appeal to the consumer. The new site I helped redesign and launch in 2015 addressed these issues and more, leading to 3x the number of unique sessions with 7+ page views per session and even a 10% increase in the average order size. After this successful revival, we continued to facelift CCB’s many other web properties.

To make the additional redesigns efficient and to keep inconsistencies at bay, I created and managed a Sketch library that was shared across the team to keep our designs in sync across multiple files. The library included reusable elements such as brand colors and default typography, buttons and form elements, and logos and icons. This proved very useful not only to the design team, but also the developers as it helped guide them to create a base CSS file with reusable HTML elements from the outset of new sites moving forward.

App Launch Brings Growth

The advantages of chat-based e-commerce allowed us to create a user journey that is much more efficient from wish to fulfillment for both the customer and L14. This led to the app exceeding expectations and has required L14 to expand their team of Lifestyle Specialists as they continue to take on more members and fill more requests than ever before. Mastercard’s UAE division has also noticed the benefits of this service and are now licensing the app to serve as concierge for its top-tier cardholders.
