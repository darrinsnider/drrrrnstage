I’m Darrin - a Los Angeles-based UX strategist who works directly with developers and stakeholders in an Agile environment to deliver high-quality, enjoyable experiences for end users. My passion lies in finding creative, user-friendly solutions to complex technology problems and furthering the personal and professional growth of both myself and my team.

what i do

01/ UX[ user experience ]

People are always the priority. I seek a deeper understanding of a product’s intent and its user’s needs to strategize the bigger picture and develop full product experiences — feature definition, user stories, wireframes, mock-ups, final UI designs — that delight the end user while aligning with business rules and KPIs.

02/ UI[ user interface ]

A pretty face will only get you so far. I create seamless interfaces and flexible design systems from adaptable components that are efficient for development and allow a product to scale. My strong attention to detail keeps elements consistent and familiar, guiding the user and preventing unexpected outcomes.

03/ PM[ project mgmtmanagement ]

A chain is only as strong as the weakest link. I work closely and build relationships with my design, development, and management team members to establish a clear understanding of project goals, timeline, and contingencies, communicating with the client to set expectations and delegating tasks to assure deadlines are met.
