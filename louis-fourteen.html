Louis Fourteen

Creating an ultra-premium chat-based concierge app.

role/

Lead UX & Visual Design

agency/

Freelance

industry/

Concierge

year/

2018

overview/

Louis Fourteen (L14) is a Dubai-based boutique lifestyle management company allowing its members to get the most out of life by organizing bespoke travel experiences, sourcing luxury goods, providing access to special events, and arranging personal shopping and styling services. To boost current member sales and expand to international clients, I designed a native mobile app to alleviate the pain points of being a phone- and email-only service and enable them grow in the digital space. The app is now being licensed by Mastercard as a concierge service for World Elite cardholders.

Less Talk, More Text

For affluent clientele that are always in business meetings or traveling on planes and in places they may not have cell service, it can be difficult to find the time to make a phone call to L14. Things get even more complicated when arranging a surprise gift or vacation, as they are always in the presence of their closest friends and loved ones. Creating a chat-based concierge app solves these problems — retaining both the level of detail needed to make these requests and the personal touch of speaking to your Lifestyle Specialist while allowing your wishes to be discussed in a more private and convenient manner.

Beyond serving members exactly what they desire through the “Ask Louis” chat feature, they are also given the opportunity to discover curated offers based on data captured in the onboarding flow as well as tags that are assigned by Lifestyle Specialists as they interact with and understand their client. This “Serendipity” feature includes the unique ability to create open offers, like reserving a table for dinner or planning a trip to Bora Bora, that route the client back to “Ask Louis” for the Lifestyle Specialist to gather the additional information required before booking.

Your every wish, granted. No matter what you desire, just ask your Louis Fourteen Lifestyle Specialist then sit back and relax while they take care of the rest.
Need a vacation but not sure where to go? The Serendipity section presents offers of all types curated for you using info gathered from your interactions with both the app and your Lifestyle Specialist.
When you book a trip or we notice you are in a new city, we surface offers related to your travel to make your stay as relaxing and enjoyable as it can be.

App Launch Brings Growth

The advantages of chat-based e-commerce allowed us to create a user journey that is much more efficient from wish to fulfillment for both the customer and L14. This led to the app exceeding expectations and has required L14 to expand their team of Lifestyle Specialists as they continue to take on more members and fill more requests than ever before. Mastercard’s UAE division has also noticed the benefits of this service and are now licensing the app to serve as concierge for its top-tier cardholders.
